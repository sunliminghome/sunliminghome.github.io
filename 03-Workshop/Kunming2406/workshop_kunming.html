Workshop on Advances in Variational Methods


Bird view of the research center

The workshop will be held June 9-15, 2024, at Tianyuan Mathematics Research Center, Kunming, Yunnan Province, China (http://tianyuan.amss.ac.cn). The workshop is sponsored by Tianyuan Fund for Mathematics.

The workshop will cover a wide range of topics, including but not limited to the classification, regularity, and quantitative property of solutions to nonlinear partial differential equations and systems.
